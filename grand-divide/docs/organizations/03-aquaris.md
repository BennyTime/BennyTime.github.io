# Aquaris, the Tideborn Empire

The oceanic empire guided by tides, currents, and ancient maritime traditions.

## Tidecourt

The spiritual and political authority governing Aquaris. Decisions are guided by ritual interpretation of tides, currents, and the Water Shard's resonance.

**Tide Regent:** Lunara Shenhai (Nerai)

The Tidecourt combines religious authority with political power, interpreting the will of the Water Shard through natural phenomena. Their decisions shape naval policy, trade routes, and diplomatic relations.

---

## Pearl Navigators

Elite navigators responsible for charting surface and abyssal routes. Their teachings allow Aquaris fleets to travel paths unknown to others.

**Navigator Prime:** Kaelis Yunwei (Nerai)

The Pearl Navigators guard the secrets of oceanic navigation, including routes through treacherous waters and passages to the Abyssal Deep. Their knowledge gives Aquaris unmatched naval superiority.

---

## Abyssal Archivists

Keepers of ancient songs, records, and prophecies dating back to the end of the Shard War. Access to their archives is tightly controlled.

**High Archivist:** Thesryn Haizhen (Nerai)

The Archivists maintain oral traditions and written records predating the Shard War's conclusion. What they know about the world's lost history remains one of Vaeloran's greatest mysteries.
