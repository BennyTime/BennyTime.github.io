# Aerion Skydrift

The floating islands nation governed by wind-callers and aerial traditions.

## Windbound Concord

The governing council of Aerion's floating islands, responsible for airspace neutrality and sky-route security.

**High Windcaller:** Aera Sahir (Dravari)

The Concord maintains Aerion's status as neutral airspace, mediating disputes over sky-routes and ensuring safe passage for all nations' airships. Their authority in aerial matters is recognized across Vaeloran.

---

## Skyward Riders

Elite aerial patrols escorting airships and hunting sky-beasts.

**Wing Captain:** Thalos Zephyrin (Dravari)

The Riders protect trade routes from sky-beasts, pirates, and atmospheric phenomena. Mounted on flying creatures or piloting wind-powered craft, they are the finest aerial combatants in Vaeloran.

---

## Windspire Academy Orders

Schools dedicated to aeromancy, sky combat, and bonded mount training. Graduates are highly sought after across Vaeloran.

**Grand Instructor:** Vaelis Aerun (Dravari)

The Academy trains mages, riders, and navigators in the arts of aerial mastery. Its graduates serve as airship pilots, weather-callers, and sky-cavalry across multiple nations, making the Academy one of Vaeloran's most influential institutions.
