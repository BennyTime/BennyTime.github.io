# Concordant Plains

The neutral diplomatic hub of Vaeloran, where representatives from all nations convene.

## Hall of Accord Council

A permanent diplomatic assembly where every recognized nation holds a seat.

**Speaker:** Eldren Maelorin (Hyran)

The Hall of Accord serves as the primary venue for international diplomacy, treaty negotiation, and conflict mediation. Every major decision affecting multiple nations passes through this council, though enforcement depends on mutual agreement.

---

## Wardens of Concord

Neutral peacekeepers tasked with enforcing the Treaty of Unity within the Concordant Plains. They are one of the few forces legally allowed to bear arms in Eldwyn Vale and may detain individuals of any status.

**High Warden:** Ser Caldeir Vaelis (Aelvar)

The Wardens maintain order in the neutral zone, ensuring that diplomatic proceedings remain peaceful. Their authority supersedes national jurisdiction within the Concordant Plains, making them both respected and feared.

---

## Adventurers' Compact

An organization that regulates adventuring through licenses, contracts, and dispute mediation. Most major cities recognize Compact seals, especially for bounties and dungeon claims.

**Compact Marshal:** Ressa Thornpaw (Therian)

**Recognition:** International

The Compact standardizes adventuring practices across Vaeloran, providing legal protection, contract enforcement, and dispute resolution. Adventurers with Compact licenses gain access to official bounties, dungeon rights, and international recognition.

---

## Scholar's Promenade Circles

Loose associations of scholars, arcanists, philosophers, and inventors operating out of Eldwyn Vale. Many influential discoveries begin here long before nations claim them.

**First Convener:** Professor Ilwen Sa'ar (Plasari)

The Promenade operates as a neutral ground for academic exchange, where researchers from all nations can collaborate without political interference. Breakthroughs made here often shape the future of entire nations.
