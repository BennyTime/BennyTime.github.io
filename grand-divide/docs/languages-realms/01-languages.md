## Extra Languages

These languages are spoken by creatures beyond the common races:

- **Daemonic** - Language of the Daemon
- **Celestial** - Language of divine beings
- **Giantkin** - Language of giants and large creatures
- **Fey Cant** - Language of the Fey Realm
- **Draconic** - Ancient language of dragons
- **Primordial** - Language of elemental beings
- **Deep Speech** - Language of aberrations and the void
- **Gutter Tongue** - Monster common tongue
